Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 5, 2025

MultiChat Profile (“the app” or “we”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does not collect, how any local data is stored, and how you can contact us.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location or device identifiers
  • Photos, contacts, messages, or other private content
  • Analytics or usage metrics

2. Local Storage

  • All profiles and settings are saved only on your device.
  • No data is ever sent to our servers or any external service.
  • Profile data is used exclusively for in-app features.

3. Security

  • Profiles can be locked with the app’s built-in PIN lock or Face ID.
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data.

4. Embedded Third-Party Chat

The app can display content from a web-based chat service within its embedded browser view. This content is provided by the external service and governed by its own terms. We do not collect or store any information from these sessions.

5. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no child-directed content.

6. Policy Updates

  • When this policy changes, the “Last updated” date will be revised.
  • Significant changes may be communicated via app update notes or in-app notices.
